Model note for students

This is an empirical log-linear prediction model based on measured data. It learns the effects of surface area, temperature and concentration together. Predictions within the measured range are more reliable. Predictions at very high temperature or very high acid concentration are extrapolations and should not be treated as safe laboratory instructions.
